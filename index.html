About me

I am a Ph.D. student in Economics at UC San Diego. My research focuses on climate change adaptation and mitigation, using tools from macroeconomics, finance, and trade. My main interests include climate finance, carbon pricing, and the energy transition. I have received the Fulbright Scholarship and the Outstanding Woman Engineer award from the University of Concepción.
