Work in Progress

Pricing and Transmission of Climate Risk in Corporate Lending (with Max Yang)

Local natural disasters are geographically concentrated shocks, yet credit markets are nationally and globally integrated, raising the question of whether physical climate risk remains local or propagates through the financial system.

Unequal Investment: How Physical Climate Risk Shapes Firm Behavior

This paper studies whether exposure to physical climate risk weakens the investment channel of monetary policy.
