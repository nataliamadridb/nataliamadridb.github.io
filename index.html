About me

I am a Fulbright scholar and a Ph.D. student in Economics at the University of California, San Diego. I am focused on making progress in adapting to and mitigating the impacts of climate change, using tools from macro, finance and trade.

I have received several notable scholarships and recognitions. These include a Fulbright Scholarship—widely regarded as one of the most prestigious scholarships globally—to pursue my Ph.D. in the United States. I was honored as an Outstanding Woman Engineer by the Faculty of Engineering at the University of Concepción. Additionally, I was awarded a scholarship from the Chilean Ministry of Education to pursue my undergraduate studies, granted in recognition of my academic excellence.
